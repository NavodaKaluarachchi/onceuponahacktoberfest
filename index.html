Once Upon a Hacktoberfest

Chapter 1 - The Giant and the Dwarf

The day had dawned clear. Perhaps not as clear as the giant that unusually burst from the ground and launched its attack on the dwarven village.
- Where are the stones? - roared the giant as he tore down the village roofs with the old trunk of a tree.
The dwarf soldiers promptly prepared the crossbows and fired at the giant, but it had no effect.
